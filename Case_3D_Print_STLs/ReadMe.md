Please ensure this is the same board. I originally made a case from a STEP of the board I found online and upon getting the board realized it was not the exact same hardware layout 
(few few mm here and there of difference) and was a different revision so I had to make revisions to the cases design. The board in which I designed the case around is from Open Source SDR Labs entitled 
"OpenSourceSDRLab New 7020-SDR AD9361 Development Board with PA" bought in 2026. A few comments about the 3D print files.

1) The design requires various hardware to anchor the board to the base and to fix the top and bottom together. I used threaded inserts to allow for easy mounting and connection.
2) For board mounting, you will need 4x M2 * 2 * 3 threaded inserts and 4 corresponding M2 * 4 bolts.
3) For connecting the top and bottom of the case you will need 4x M3 * 5 * 5 threaded inserts and 4 M3 * 6 bolts
4) Finally, I included cut-outs in the top for a fan. The fan I used was bought from amazon and was intended for a RPI 3B but worked nicely. The hardware that came with the fans (it was a 2 pack) also worked
   nicely. If you are interested or want reference they can be found at the following link. https://a.co/d/0dxboW4R
   The dim of the fan is 30mm x 30mm x 7mm.
   NOTE: One adjustment I made to the fan was replacing the gpio pin connectors with the matching JST 1.25 mm connector. (I had this laying around so I cant provide a direct link but I
   would say the connector at the following link would be a good lead to finding what you might need: https://www.digikey.com/short/j9b2vf02... ADDITIONALLY if you want to buy a 30 mm x 30 mm fan with
   this connector and skip the soldering my eye (and a little research) tells me this would be what you want: https://www.digikey.com/short/thf97fhz

NOTE: If you have never used 3D Inserts in your 3D prints you will need a soldering Iron to press them into place. The inserts I used came from a kit sold on amazon at : https://a.co/d/07en3sjy
also these are not affiliate links, just to be helpful.
